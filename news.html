Honorable Mention for our short 'Domino'

We are pleased to share that our short film 'Domino' received a Honourable Mention at Los Angeles Film & Documentary Awards.

This short film was developed by The Tulips Productions in collaboration with director duo Larsen & Pickstock and shot in Almeria desert in late 2023.

Producer: Karin S. de Boer

Starring: Tony Corvillo

Directors: Nikolaj Larsen & Duncan Pickstock

Director of Photography: Paul Bessa

Sound Recordist: Curro Burguillo

Composer: Xavier Capellas

Thanks to the wonderful crew from Almeria and the municipality of Tabernas.


In the near future Tony traffics European migrants who seek a better life in Rhea – a private city state for the super rich in tempered sub-Saharian highlands. When he is forced by his furious boss to go back into the desert to look for a migrant he has abandoned, his life begins to unravel.
